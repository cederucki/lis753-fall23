    About

    We have over 50,000 wonderful used books, records, videos, magazines, and more at our monthly sale. These are mostly donations from individuals, estates, and companies. We also sell items that the Palo Alto Library has removed from their shelves to make room for our new purchases.

    You'll find these same low prices on all the other tes of thousands of books that we have. While some booksellers may pledge a portion of your purchase to help local libraries, we give 100%. Plus, we charge no sales tax and we have the friendliest sales people you'll ever find.

    Sale Rooms

    Main Room

    The main room is along the north driveway, adjacent to the Charleston shopping center. This room has hardcover and paperback books. There is a 12 book per person limit in the main room on Saturday before noon or while there is still a line for entry. This is also where you can bring large book donations.

    Children's Room

    The children's room is near the Cubberley track, pavilion, and Greendell School. During the first hour of the sale on Saturday, there is a 12 book per customer limit. This room has children's books, music, videos, and toys, including:

    • Picture Books
    • School-Age Fiction and Nonfiction
    • Non-English Language Books
    • CDs and DVDs
    • Books for Parents and Teachers

    Bargain Room

    The bargain room is in rooms K6 and K7. On Sunday, prices in the bargain room are half-off and you can save even more by purchasing grocery bags from us. The bags are $5 each and you can fill them with as many books as you can.

    FAQs

    1. What portion of proceeds supports the Palo Alto Library?

      100%

    2. Is sales tax charged?

      No

    3. How many customers are allowed inside the main room at once?

      160

    4. How is the orer customers may enter the main room determined?

      Starting at 8 AM on Saturday, numbered tickets will be given out. There is a limit of 2 tickets per person. The number on your ticket determines your place in line. If your number is called and you are not present, you will forfeit your place in line. If all tickets are handed out, a physical line will form.

    5. How many books may a customer purchase at a time in the main room?

      12, unless it is after noon and there is no line

    6. May a customer re-enter the main room after making their initial purchase?

      Yes, they may re-join the line for entry to the main room.

    7. What time does the sale start?

      Tickets for the main room will be handed out beginning at 8 AM on Saturday. The children's room and bargain room open 1 hour before the main room on Saturday.

    8. Where should large book donations be brought?

      The main room

    9. How much do books cost?

      Books usually cost $0.50 for paperbacks and $1 for hardcovers. The bargain room has some items for as little as $0.25. On Sunday, prices in the bargain room are half-off, plus you can save even more by purchasing grocery bags from us for $5 and then filling those with books.

    10. Are gift certificates available?

      Yes, you can purchase gift certificates at the sale in $5 denominations
